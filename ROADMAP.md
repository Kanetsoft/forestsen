# 18-Month Development Roadmap for Forestsen

## Phase 0: Initial Planning (Months 1-3)
- **Milestones**
  - Complete project scope definition
  - Stakeholder identification and engagement
- **Deliverables**
  - Project charter
  - Initial stakeholder report
- **KPIs**
  - Stakeholder satisfaction rating > 80%
- **Budget Allocation**
  - $10,000
- **Success Criteria**
  - Approval of project charter by stakeholders
- **Risk Management Strategy**
  - Regular stakeholder meetings to assess risks

## Phase 1: Development and Prototyping (Months 4-9)
- **Milestones**
  - Completion of initial prototypes
  - User testing sessions
- **Deliverables**
  - Prototype versions of core functionalities
- **KPIs**
  - User satisfaction rating > 75% from tests
- **Budget Allocation**
  - $50,000
- **Success Criteria**
  - Positive feedback from user testing
- **Risk Management Strategy**
  - Iterative development to incorporate user feedback

## Phase 2: Beta Launch (Months 10-15)
- **Milestones**
  - Release beta version
  - Collect user feedback for improvements
- **Deliverables**
  - Beta version of the product
- **KPIs**
  - Engagement metrics (active users, feedback score > 70%)
- **Budget Allocation**
  - $30,000
- **Success Criteria**
  - Achieve at least 500 active users
- **Risk Management Strategy**
  - Contingency plan for critical feedback or bugs

## Phase 3: Full Launch and Growth (Months 16-18)
- **Milestones**
  - Launch full version
  - Marketing and outreach initiatives
- **Deliverables**
  - Official product launch
- **KPIs**
  - User growth rate > 20% month-over-month
- **Budget Allocation**
  - $40,000
- **Success Criteria**
  - Achievement of user acquisition targets
- **Risk Management Strategy**
  - Monitor marketing effectiveness and adjust strategies
